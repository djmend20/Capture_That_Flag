# Capture_That_Flag
Targeted Audience: People wanting to uphold Social Distancing or People who have no friends

First accept the use of location. Then click play. You will be brought to a screen where in the 
top left corner saids Begin which is a button, the middle is ? which will be your timer, RESET another button to erase all progress that was made in the previous game. Closer to the bottom of the screen you will see Flags Recieved text and below colored flags. After pressing Begin, the timer starts counting down and you will have 30 minutes to collect all the flags else you lose. After approaching 20 meters of the flag, the flag will appear below the text of Flags recieved and you will need to continue on.
